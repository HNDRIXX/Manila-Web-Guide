Intramuros
Intramuros or also known as the "Walled City" in Manila, located at the south of Pasig River and east of Manila Bay was established by the Spaniards 400 years ago as their political and military base in Asia. It is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Manila, the capital of the Philippines.
