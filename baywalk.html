Manila Baywalk
Manila Baywalk is a popular recreational destination and favored sunset spot. The 2 km strip of beach extends past the Manila Yacht Club along Roxas Boulevard. It used to be a place to avoid pickpockets and other nastiness, but now it has been changed to the point that you wouldn't recognize it. The crumbling sidewalk is paved with tiles, the damaged lamp posts have been upgraded with colorful lamps, giving it a well-deserved makeover.

The Manila Baywalk has most of the relaxing things to do, such as outdoor dining. The entire area is a hotspot for a variety of dishes, including Thai, Korean, Middle Eastern, And Western and fast food street food. Sports and health enthusiasts, cyclists, jogging and walkers prefer this area.
