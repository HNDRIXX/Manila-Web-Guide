Fort Santiago
Fort Santiago and its beautifully manicured gardens on the Pasig River's bank is one of Intramuros' most popular tourist attractions, providing a welcome respite from the hustle and bustle of the metropolis. The citadel, which was established by the Spaniards and now features gardens, plazas, fountains, and a lily pond was originally built by the Spanish in 1590.
