Anthropology
The National Museum of Anthropology houses the anthropology and archaeology divisions of the National Museum of the Philippines. It was formerly known as the Museum of the Filipino People. It is located near the National Museum of Fine Arts and is just facing the National Museum of National History.

Its aim is to preserve the cultural heritage of the Philippines and to educate people. Its permanent collection contains around 10,000 items. The museum contains things and artifacts related to archaeology, prehistoric societies, languages, customs, traditions, and culture of the Philippines.

This museum is really a must visit place, you will surely gain good experience and memories.
