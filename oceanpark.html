Ocean Park
Manila Ocean Park is an oceanarium in Manila and Philippine's first world-class marine theme park and a premiere educational facility. It is home to 14,000 sea creatures from 277 species which are all indigenous to the Philippines and South East Asia.

Manila Ocean Park hosts an array of exciting attractions, enticing view of the bay, enriching marine life exhibits, entertaining dining and lifestyle shapes to mold into an integrated resort destination.

In the evening, lovers, young and old, will discover a romantic environment as well as peace within the park's surroundings. There are numerous attractions within the park, and it takes at least a day to completely explore and appreciate them all.
