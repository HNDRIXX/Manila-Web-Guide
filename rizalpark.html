Rizal Park
Rizal Park, often known as Luneta Park or just Luneta, is a historic urban park in Manila. It is regarded as one of Asia's most beautiful urban parks. The park's location was known as Bagumbayan during the Spanish colonial period, and it played an essential role in shaping the Philippines' history.

The park now attracts a diverse group of people, ranging from early morning joggers, cyclists, aerobic and martial arts enthusiasts, and families on a day outing. Vendors of all kinds may be seen all across the park, selling literally everything from bright and colorful toys to different snacks.

In the evening, lovers, young and old, will discover a romantic environment as well as peace within the park's surroundings. There are numerous attractions within the park, and it takes at least a day to completely explore and appreciate them all.
